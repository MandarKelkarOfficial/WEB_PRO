About Us

Know Us Better


We are a leading coaching center specializing in NATA & JEE Paper 2 preparation. Our focus is on nurturing students' critical thinking abilities rather than dictating what to think. Our approach includes regular cyclic mock tests, designed to assess students' mastery and grasp of the subjects. Our faculty and advisers are qualified, practicing architects, ensuring top-tier instruction. Introducing 'PRISM,' a team of multidisciplinary professionals committed to delivering premier training and personalized support for NATA and JEE Paper 2 entrance exams. Drawing upon our extensive expertise and unwavering dedication, we offer a unique one-on-one mentoring experience. Our mission is to help you establish a robust knowledge foundation for successful exam performance. We foster an exceptional learning environment guided by devoted, highly experienced mentors. Our comprehensive coaching program covers entrance exams for Architecture and Design schools. In an era where Architecture and Design are burgeoning career paths, especially for students pursuing 10+2, HSC, or equivalent exams, we are here to guide you towards a successful future.
